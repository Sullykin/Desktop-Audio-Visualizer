1. Go into the VBCABLE_Driver_Pack43 folder, and run the appropriate setup application for your computer (most likely VBCABLE_Setup_x64) as administrator.

2. Go to your sound settings and open the Sound Control Panel.

3. Set CABLE Input (VB-Audio virtual Cable) as the default playback device.

4. Go to the properties of CABLE Output (VB-Audio virtual Cable), go to listen, check "Listen to this device", and choose your normal speakers/headset as the playback device.


*All this virtual audio cable does it reroute audio from your desktop to your speakers as well as this application. 
 You can still use your mic and speakers normally.
