1. Go to https://vb-audio.com/Cable/ and install the virtual audio cable.

2. Go to your sound settings and open the Sound Control Panel.

3. Set CABLE Input as the default playback device.

4. Go to the properties of CABLE Output, go to listen, check "Listen to this device", and choose your normal speakers/headset as the playback device.


*The virtual audio cable reroutes audio from your desktop to your speakers as well as this application. 
 You can still use your mic and speakers normally.
